The Now Phenomenon!

The idea behind a /now page

Posted on September 14, 2016

So, What are you up to? , What are you doing now?, What am I doing now?

These questions haunt me every day, gnawing at my consciousness. They come in many forms, but no matter what disguise they take, they always bring forth the fear,the anxiety, the self-doubt that.. that, wait, I think its just me!

Anyway, so after getting fed up of repeatedly coming up with new things to cover up my embarrassing habit of binge watching youtube videos, I finally added a /now page to this blog.

A simple link. Easy to remember. Easy to type.

Along with being a one-stop shop for anyone who wishes to know what I'm up to, this page also acts as a nice reminder for myself, when I'm feeling unfocused. A public declaration of priorities. Something that I can be held accountable for.

So I urge you to join in the party and let me know, what are you up to these days??

P.S. : This idea is inspired by Derek Sivers.
